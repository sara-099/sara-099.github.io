About Us

Sara Mallick

I can still picture the occasion that gave me the inspiration for BeeU. When I was younger, either because of Pityriasis Alba or acne, my skin would be in horrible shape, and when I would look online for skincare products, I would discover that they were excessively expensive and would use misleading tactics to entice clients. On that day, I made the decision to dedicate myself to helping everyone who shares my enthusiasm for self-care.

A tireless mindset, numerous sleepless nights, and a fierce desire to outperform my rivals were all part of the development process. To make sure that my website is appropriate and appealing to my target audience, I manually coded everything which added a heavy weight on my workload. My aim was obvious, but the journey was difficult since I entered an industry that was very competitive. I felt demotivated, stumbled, and made mistakes yet I persisted in going forward. After many days of effort and hard work, BeeU was finally materialized on 27 April 2023, and so did my desire to diligently and unceasingly serve my BeeU community.
